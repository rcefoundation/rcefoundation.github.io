About the Foundation

The first random chimp event occured on 19-1-2009, and its unprofessional cover-up by the government led to the immediate formation of the Anti-Chimp Council. However, the events unpredictably stopped occuring, and the Council was soon dissolved.

However, a resurgence of Random Chimp Events on 17-02-2020 has convinced the Council Elders to re-activate the Council under a new name, the Random Chimp Event Foundation.

The aim of the Foundation is to keep mankind informed of the threat of the Events and prevent any further Event from occuring.
